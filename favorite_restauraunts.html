Welcome

This site was created to disaply my favorite restauraunts located in Phoenix, Arizona.

1. Oh-Ya Sushi

Located in Glendale, Arizona, Oh-Ya Sushi is a Japanese and Korean eatery. I've only ever tried their sushi, though it's some of the best sushi I have eaten.

2. The Cracked Egg

When looking for a breakfast spot with great customer service and amazing pancakes, The Cracked Egg is your go to location! My favorite dish is their waffles.

3. Oregano's Pizza Bistro

Oregano's is my go to location for pasta and pizza. My favorite dish is their chicken piccata!

4. IKEA Food & Swedish Marketplace

Hear me out, IKEA's meatballs are incredible. Their chicken tenders are incredible. Everything is incredible! It's even better after you spend hundreds of dollars on furniture. However, IKEA is located in Tempe, Arizona, so it is bit of a drive.

5. R.Kidds Pizza & Wings

Home of the best hawaiian pizza. Their wings are delicious and crispy.
